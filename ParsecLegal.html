PARSEC Roleplaying Game – Open Content Declaration

The entire text of the PARSEC core rules (character creation, task resolution, starship design, combat, psionics, world generation, etc.) is declared Open Game Content and may be used under the Open Game License v1.0a (full text in Appendix).

The following are declared Product Identity and are NOT open:

  • The name “PARSEC” and the PARSEC logo
  • All artwork, trade dress, and layout
  • All setting lore, example fiction, and proper names created for this book
  • The terms “Parsec Scale”, “Quantum Drift”, “Voidborn” (or whatever cool terms you invent)

You are free to create and sell your own adventures, hacks, and supplements if you follow the OGL and do not use Product Identity.
